Why a Two-Agent System?
7
Single Monolithic System 
• Difficult to scale
• Hard to debug and maintain
Two-Agent System (DatasetAgent + AnalystAgent)• Single responsibility per agent• Deterministic pipeline: numbers are computed, never generated

===== SLIDE 19 =====
What Makes DataVerse Different
6
• Retail-domain guard detects and refuses non-retail datasets before storage
• Every number is computed by Pandas and scikit-learn, never invented by an LLM
• Verifiable receipts: Show the math on any KPI in one click
• SHA-256 reproducibility certificate seals and re-verifies each analysis
• Two-agent pipeline: DatasetAgent validates, AnalystAgent analyzes
• Explainable AI: SHAP with deterministic fallback plus counterfactuals
• DeepAnalyze-8B supported as the preferred agentic reasoning engine
